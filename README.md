# Zhouli-Data-Analysis
The Zhouli, or Rights of the Zhou, is a 2nd BC description of court organization and bureacracy in ancient China.  It contains something like a spreadsheet of staff, jobs, departments, which I have analyzed.
I used rudimentary Japanese to identify simple Chinese characters indicating numbers (1-9) and person.  AI, with context, was able to translate some of the Chinese, specifically the "spreadsheet" sections.
Numbers of people are easier to translate than the actual descriptions of duties, ceremonies, etc.
